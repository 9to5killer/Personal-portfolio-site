# Meeting Intelligence Agent

An AI system that generates structured summaries, action items, and decision logs from meeting transcripts.

## What it does

Built on Whisper for transcription and Claude for analysis, this agent processes meeting recordings and produces:

- Structured summaries with key discussion points
- Action items attributed to specific participants
- Decision logs with context and rationale
- Follow-up questions that went unresolved

## Why I'm building it

Most enterprise meeting waste isn't from too many meetings — it's from meetings without memory. Decisions get made but not recorded. Actions get assigned but not tracked. This agent closes that loop.
