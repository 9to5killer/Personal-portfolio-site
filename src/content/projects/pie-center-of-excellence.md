# Center of Excellence — AI Agent Pipeline

## The problem

PIE runs executive peer communities for enterprise clients — 6,000+ executive interviews annually. Directors and Managing Directors earning $100K–$250K were spending 20+ minutes per interview on note cleanup and Salesforce data entry. Every major client deliverable and internal workflow was manual.

At that volume, that equals 800+ hours of senior capacity consumed by administrative work every year — time that should have been spent on client relationships and revenue activity.

## The system

A 10+ agent pipeline automating the full client delivery operation — every major client deliverable and every internal workflow required to produce them, from interview intelligence through thought leadership, sales enablement, and business development.

**AI note taker integration** — Integrated into Salesforce, eliminated manual data entry for recorded interviews entirely.

**Interview note cleaner agent** — Cleans raw manual notes from sensitive-industry interviews, saves 40%+ processing time per interview.

**Agenda creator agent** — Synthesizes all interview notes across a community cohort into a prioritized roundtable agenda.

**Facilitation guide builder** — Creates a facilitator script with per-executive cues based on individual interview notes.

**Executive summary producer** — Converts roundtable transcripts into publishable thought leadership pieces.

**Participant summary producer** — Breaks transcripts down by member and surfaces selling opportunities per account.

**Top theme tracker agent** — Extracts top 3 themes per interview with attributed quotes structured for sales and marketing use.

**Interview to action report agent** — Consolidates interview notes, theme tracking, and participant summaries into a single account debrief report for sellers.

**BD prep agent** — Generates a real-time prospect intelligence brief before business development meetings.

**BD proposal agent** — Converts raw meeting notes into structured proposals.

**Recruiting agent** — Drafts personalized outreach for executive community recruitment.

## Outcomes

- 800+ hours of Director and Managing Director time recovered annually
- 40%+ reduction in post-interview processing time
- Salesforce data entry fully automated for recorded interviews — zero manual input required
- Full client delivery operation running at higher volume, higher quality, and faster turnaround without additional headcount
- Clients receive more deliverables, more consultative insight, and more selling opportunities identified per engagement cycle
- Senior staff capacity redirected from administrative work to client-facing revenue activity

## What I learned

Not every interview can be recorded. Sensitivity constraints across industries require two parallel ingestion paths — automated and manual — that produce consistent output from both.

The outcome was intentional Jevons Paradox: same team, higher volume, higher quality, no additional hires. Force multiplication, not replacement.

The real unlock was treating every client deliverable as a structured output problem, not a creative one — once you define the output precisely, the agent almost writes itself.
