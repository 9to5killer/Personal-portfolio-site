# Knowledge Worker Augmentation Suite

A collection of AI tools targeting specific workflows where professional expertise creates the most value — and where manual effort creates the most drag.

## Planned modules

- **Research synthesis**: Aggregate and structure research across documents, transcripts, and data
- **Draft reviewer**: Structured feedback on professional writing against domain criteria
- **Expertise capture**: Interview-based knowledge extraction for institutional memory

## Philosophy

These tools are designed to augment, not replace. Each one keeps professional judgment in the loop while removing the parts of the work that don't require it.

## Status

Planned. Backlog prioritized pending Meeting Intelligence Agent launch.
